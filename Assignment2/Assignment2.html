The light bulb which is widely known created by Thomas Alva Edison had lighted houses of billions and have been evolving ever since. It converts electric energy to light that makes dark places visible. This have a huge effect on the society because humans are able to work at night to speed up the revolution of the nation.

Besides that, light bulb also play a huge role in our daily lives. Lights are used outside to make things visible and keep people safe. You can see light bulbs in street lamps, at bus stops, in parking lots and at the gas station, its hood aglow with bright fluorescent bulbs plastering the area above your head.

These days, light bulb has evolved into different kind of types of light bulb such as LED, fluorescent and incandescent lights. The city that used the most light bulb which is Las Vegas is a testament for the allure of light bulb; Some casinos have thousands constantly flashing to draw customers their way. The Luxor's famous light beam shoots straight up into the sky with the power of 39 Xenon light bulbs. The Neon Museum in Las Vegas houses a graveyard of old signs, which used neon bulbs to garner tourist attention.
