Our History

The business began in 1990, with a bakery opened by the world's most renowned pastry chef Scott Mccrindle in Barrie, Ontario. His introduction of the "Sell in Bulk System" caught attention of many and the business quickly expanded. With the wide usage of internet in 2000, Scott strategically move the business online, making the cookies more available for the public.
