History of Cookies

The word "cookie" as we know them stands for "little cake" in Dutch. Cookies exist about 1400 years ago and ever since has become a famous home delicacy enjoyed by everyone. Cookies are so beloved in modern history that most home cooks include them in their repertoire. The range of cookie types quickly expanded with different kind of flavours.
